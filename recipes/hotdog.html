Hotdog

The perfect hotdog

A hotdog doesn't require much at all, anyone can afford to make one. This simply makes them perfect.

ingredients

  • Hotdogs
  • Buns
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Grab a hotdog bun
  2. Add the sausage
  3. Squirt some mustard and ketchup on
  4. (optional) Add some fried onions
