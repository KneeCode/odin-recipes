BLT Sandwich

Crunchy, salty, it's just good.

The BLT sandwich is best when crafted with fresh ingredients, especially the three main ingredients. Can you guess what they are?

ingredients

  1. Grab a single piece of toasted white bread
  2. Add mayonaisse to entire slice
  3. Make a bottom layer of lettuce
  4. Add tomato on top of lettuce
  5. Top it of with crunchy bacon
  6. Add mayonaisse to the second piece of toasted white bread
  7. Squash it all together
